About Me
From a young age, I've been fascinated with language and writing. This passion for language has followed me throughout my career, from a Bachelor's degree in Communication Studies and a Graduate Certificate in Technical Communication to my current job at the University of Minnesota. I use my communication skills every day as a Graduate Program Coordinator and Executive Accounts Specialist at the UMN, where I create how-to guides and overview documents for work processes in the ISyE Department.
I've always admired how effective communication skills make it possible for people to exchange knowledge and build connections. When it comes to my own writing, I consistently strive for clarity, readability, and accessibility. Some of my past projects are available below, including projects I created for my Technical Communication degree as well as work samples from the University of Minnesota.
