COVID Vaccination Requirement How-To Guide
I created a how-to guide for new employees in my department to explain how to complete the COVID Vaccination Requirement for new hires.
Context
In my job at the University of Minnesota, I act as the payroll contact for the ISyE Department. I am responsible for ensuring that all new hires complete their required paperwork.
The University of Minnesota requires that all new employees provide proof of COVID vaccination or request an exemption. The instructions on how to complete this requirement are separated across several different University web pages, so I decided to create a how-to guide that would consolidate all of the necessary information for new employees in my department.
Process
I created this document from scratch, and I began by researching the information on the vaccination requirements listed across the different University web pages.
Once I had consolidated all of the information, I worked on rewriting the instructions to address the user directly. I also included screenshots of the online interface for new hires to follow along.
Impact
This document filled a gap in my department's work processes. Previously, when we hired new employees, I had to explain the COVID vaccination requirement through a lengthy email or over a Zoom call.
My how-to guide explains the process clearly and concisely. It also presents a professional image to new employees who are just joining the University of Minnesota.
